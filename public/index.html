Content Creation and Production

Work

We write, direct and produce video content for medium and large enterprises. We are based in Switzerland, but we collaborate globally. Our strong suit is the tricky stuff: If your project is complex and a lot is at stake, we are the right guys.

In the past we have also made a number of really short short films, like VAND and Wired, and a few more involved projects, like Birds and Dice. Right now we are working on a TV series about human trafficking, called Alina.

About

We are a duo of filmmakers who cover a range of skills: Philipp is the man with the creative vision – he writes, directs and edits.

Manuel is the guy who keeps things in order – he produces, organizes and does the tech stuff.

With 200+ client projects and 8 short films under our shared belt now, we continue building Rise and Shine Films on the same key principles since 2013: 1. Every project is unique, 2. More isn't always better and 3. Don't panic.
